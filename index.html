Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for the way you actually work — quick access to everything you've copied, always within reach.

Auto-saves everything

Every text you copy is automatically captured and stored — no manual action needed.

50-item history

Browse and manage your last 50 clipboard items in a clean, scrollable list.

One-tap reuse

Tap any saved item to instantly copy it back to your clipboard. No friction.

No duplicates

Smart deduplication keeps your history clean — copy the same text twice, stored only once.

One-tap delete

Remove items you no longer need with a single tap. Keep your history tidy.

Background service

Runs reliably in the background, capturing clips even when the app isn't open.
